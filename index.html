About Me

Xu Liu received the B.E. degree in electronic and information engineering from Liaoning Technical University, Huludao, China, with a minor in data science and big data technology (Tencent Premier Class) from Tencent Cloud Computing (Beijing) Company Ltd, Shanghai Motong Huakai Education Technology Company Ltd., and Liaoning Technical University, Huludao, China, jointly, in 2022. He received the M.E. degree in information and communication engineering from Hefei University of Technology, Hefei, China, in 2025. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in intelligence science and technology with the Shanghai Key Laboratory of Wearable Robotics and Human-Machine Interaction, and Shanghai Research Institude for Intelligent Autonomous Systems, Tongji University, Shanghai, China. In 2025, he was honored with a scholarship awarded by Prof. Tat-Seng Chua, a Fellow of the Singapore National Academy of Science. His research interests include deep learning, vision, embodied intelligence, and robotics.
